Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery charges?

Delivery is free for orders above Rs. 1000 within a 5km radius. For orders below Rs. 1000, we charge Rs. 50. Delivery to areas beyond 5km may incur additional charges based on distance.

How can I pay for my order?

We accept cash on delivery, digital payments (eSewa, Khalti, IME Pay), and credit facilities for approved regular customers. For phone orders, payment can be made upon delivery.

Do you offer credit facilities?

Yes, we offer monthly credit facilities to trusted regular customers. Please visit our store with your citizenship card and a reference to apply. Credit terms are flexible based on your purchasing history.

Can I place orders over the phone?

Absolutely! You can call us at +977 9808559761 to place your order. For faster service, regular customers can message their orders on WhatsApp at +977 9849344113.

Do you have special offers for senior citizens?

Yes, we offer a 5% discount for senior citizens (65+ years) on all grocery items. We also provide priority service and assistance with carrying groceries for our elderly customers.

What if I receive a damaged product?

We guarantee the quality of all our products. If you receive any damaged or expired product, please contact us immediately. We will replace the item free of charge or provide a full refund.
