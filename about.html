About Shabi Kirana & Khaja Ghar

Our story of serving the community with quality products and heartfelt service

Our Humble Beginnings

Serving the lamsalbesi community since 2010

Shabi Kirana & Khaja Ghar started as a small neighborhood store with a simple mission: to provide fresh, quality groceries and homemade snacks to our local community. What began as a modest kirana store has grown into a beloved local institution, known not just for our products but for the personal connections we've built with our customers.

In 2010, we expanded our services to include a tea shop and khaja ghar (snack corner), responding to our customers' requests for a place to enjoy a quick, delicious meal or a refreshing cup of tea. Today, our tea is famous throughout the area, and our chowmin has become a local favorite.

Despite our growth, we've never lost sight of our roots. We remain a family-run business committed to the values that have guided us from the beginning: quality, authenticity, and community.

Our Journey

2010

Shabi Kirana opens its doors as a small grocery store serving the lamsalbesi community with basic essentials and household items.

2012

Expanded product range to include fresh vegetables and fruits based on customer requests. Introduced monthly credit system for trusted customers.

2015

Launched the tea shop and khaja ghar, offering black tea, milk tea, and simple snacks. Quickly became a community gathering spot.

2018

Introduced homemade snacks and expanded tea varieties. Our chowmin became an instant hit among customers.

2022

Started home delivery service for orders above Rs. 1000. Expanded store space to accommodate growing product range.

2025

Celebrated 15 years of serving the community. Continued to adapt to changing customer needs while maintaining our core values.

Our Community Impact

At Shabi Kirana & Khaja Ghar, we believe in giving back to the community that has supported us for nearly two decades. Beyond being a place to shop, we've become a community hub where neighbors connect and relationships flourish.

We support local suppliers and farmers, sourcing fresh produce directly from our farms. During festivals and community events, we actively participate and contribute to making lamsalbesi a vibrant place to live.

Our tea shop has become more than just a place to grab a drink—it's where friendships are formed, news is shared, and the community comes together. We're proud to play this role in our neighborhood.
