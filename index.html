Our Story

Serving the community since 2010

Shabi Kirana and Khaja Ghar has been a trusted name for quality groceries, delicious tea, and homemade snacks in Mandandeupur. We take pride in offering personalized service and quality products.

Our tea shop has become a popular gathering spot for locals who enjoy our freshly prepared black tea, milk tea, and white tea along with snacks like chowmin and other local delicacies.
