Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM, seven days a week. Orders placed before 4:00 PM will be delivered the same day.

How can I pay for my order?

We accept cash on delivery, digital payments (eSewa, Khalti), and credit facilities for approved customers.

Do you deliver outside Lamsalbesi?

Our free delivery service covers areas within 5km of our store. For deliveries beyond this radius, additional charges apply based on distance.

How do I apply for credit facility?

Please visit our store with your citizenship card and a reference. After verification, we can set up a credit account for you.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your order within 30 minutes of placing it by calling us directly.
